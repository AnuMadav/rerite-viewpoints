re-rite@viewpoints is a current affairs marketplace. This marketplace caters to learners, academicians, policy makers, bureaucrats, civil society and aspirants for competitive examinations. A keen researcher or an avid reader could consider creating collectibles on his/her 'topic of interest' for purchase from this marketplace. A collectible is an assorted group of items collected under a particular topic. The assorted group of items collected under a collectible could include snippets from newspapers, an overview of relevant research articles, charts/ graphs associated to policies and various perspectives offered by contemporary debates.
